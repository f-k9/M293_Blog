Side Dishes

Hummus

Hummus is an oriental specialty made from pureed chickpeas that's eaten around the Middle East.

Baba Ghanoush

Baba Ghanoush is a puree of Arabic cuisine made from eggplant and sesame paste, served as a dip or side dish.

Kibbeh

Kibbeh, also spelled kebbe, is the name for a side dish widely eaten in the Middle East. They're meaty dumplings made out of bulgur.
