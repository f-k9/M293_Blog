Main Dishes

Chicken Shawarma

Shawarma is a meat dish of Arabic cuisine. It is mainly eaten in Lebanon, Syria, Jordan and Egypt.

Muluchiya

Muluchiya is a popular dish from Arabic cuisine. It consists of the green spinach-like leaves of Corchorus olitorius.

Riz A Djej

Served at every occasion and gathering, this Lebanese chicken and rice dish is a classic. The Rice is served shredded chicken.

Warak Enab

Warak Enab in Arabic, is a Lebanese dish of spiced rice and beef rolled in grape leaves and simmered in a Lemony broth.
